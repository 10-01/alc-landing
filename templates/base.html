How it works

We provide a medium for people to connect through an unexpected gift at an unexpected time.

We will contribute up to $30 to each gift at no cost to you.

Locate

Check the map to see if we’re near a loved one.

Select

Request a gift you’d like us to bring to them (food, flowers, pranks, anything).

Enjoy

Watch the video, view the pics and read our post to see how they reacted, then share it!

We are traveling the country, connecting those who are distance-challenged.
With the delivery of a small gift, we help you surprise your friends and family who live far away.
All we ask in return is the opportunity to learn more about this country from the great people who live in it.
￼￼We provide both the ‘delivery' component and the financial commitment of $30 per week to the gift submission of our choice.
And yes, we will record and post the responses of the recipients so you can see it all happen.
